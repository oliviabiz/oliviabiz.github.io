the love we have always deserved

2023

by olivia biz


The room is a reminder to both the child looking up and the adult looking back, that we are and have always been worthy of this love, praise, and adoration.

The linear temporalities of queer and trans journeys are obscured in this exhibition, as we converse with the children still within us. Viewers are invited to step into the “bedroom”, take their shoes off, crawl onto the carpet and allow themselves to dream big. Across the walls are posters of the idols I would have needed to see growing up: portraits of real QTBIPOC adults proudly living out their dreams. The TV plays a recollection of these dreams: the ones we had as children and the ones we have now.
